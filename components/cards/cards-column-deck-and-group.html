Cards column, deck and group

UiBibz::Ui::Core::Cards::CardColumn, UiBibz::Ui::Core::Cards::CardDeck, UiBibz::Ui::Core::Cards::CardGroup

The card_column component is an extension of component element.
Like this one, the card_column component has options and html_options present as hash.
It inherits options of component element.

Card column, card deck and card group contains cards.
